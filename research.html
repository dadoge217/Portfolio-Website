Political AI (September 2024-May 2025)

The goal of this research was to create an LLM-based AI model that could remove bias from bias inputs. This would make it easier to maintain factual information with limited input. During the research, I helped test the model and adjust it to maintain neutrality.

Dog Pose Estimation (September 2025-Present)

The current goal of this research is to expand on existing smart feeder technology to make them correctly react and observe dog behavior using marker-based deep learning. This model will be able to accurately observe and notify owners of current dog activity when away. I developed and implemented a UDP-based multi camera recording system to capture, display, and record dog videos.
