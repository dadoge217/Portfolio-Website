A Day In The Life

In my free time, I love to bake, woodcarve, and go to the many museums of the world. Perhaps in another life I would have pursued one of these hobbies into a full-time career. However, my love of both money and creating led me to go into computer science.

At Kent, I spend most of my time working, although I am a proud member of the planetarium club. I hope that I can eventually become a teacher because my hobbies and clubs really inspire me to want to inspire others.
